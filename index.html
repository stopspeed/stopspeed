About Us

The organization is formed in the memory of Late Sriharsha, who was a victim of road accident. Mrs. Himabindu Reddy, the founder of the foundation is committed on taking STOP SPEEDING awareness across India. She is in unbearable pain of losing her son and want contribute her services to stop such incidents however the way possible by her. Death toll in road accidents are increasing gradually every year in India. Youth are the majority victims in these cases. Racing is a passion to many youngsters in this country, to aware them about the value of life, Mrs Himabindu has come up with the concept "STOP SPEEDING". this campaign is planned to conduct in all the 29 states of India. As a start, the foundation is conducting the first season in Hyderabad, Telangana.
Hero NagaChaitanya, who is a well known cine star in Tollywood and a racer himself has tied up with Stop Speeding campaign, in which he will drive on the roads to create awareness. This campaign will showcase the after effects of speed racing, could be death, injuries or traumas. parents of deceased, victims will participate in the campaign. Sriharsha Foundation is doing this event in association with Telangana Police dept and Ministry of Youth affairs.
